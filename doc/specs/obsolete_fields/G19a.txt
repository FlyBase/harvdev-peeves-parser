
## `! G19a. Gene order/orientation from molecular mapping [SoftCV] *s :`

### Multiple values allowed:

Yes

### !c Applicable:

Yes

### Inclusion essential:

No

### Mandatorially has value:

No

### Checks:

List of valid gene symbols (either pre-existing or defined in curation record)
each suffixed with one of +, - or ? and separated either by a single space or the string
" = ".

Must be prefixed by one of two SoftCV terms:
' Gene order: In direction of increasing cytology: '
'Gene order: Overall orientation not stated: '
(Note the trailing space after the second : in each of the above.)
